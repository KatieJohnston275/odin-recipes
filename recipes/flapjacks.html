    • A recipe to make the gooiest flapjacks ever!

    Ingredients

    • 250g porridge oats
    • 125g butter
    • 125g light brown sugar
    • 2-3tbsp golden syrup
    • 100g glace cherries

    Method

    1. Preheat the oven to 180c and line a 20x20cm baking tray
    2. Melt the butter and golden syrup in a pan
    3. Stir in the sugar until melted but not boiling
    4. add wet ingredients to dry ingredients and mix well
    5. add glace cherries nd stir
    6. smooth mixture into the baking tine and bake for 15 mins
