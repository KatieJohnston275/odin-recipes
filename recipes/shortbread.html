Doggy Shortbread

  • Home

A recipe to make shortbread biscuits that look good enough for your doggy!

Ingredients

  • 125g butter
  • 55g caster sugar
  • 180g plain flour
  • 1tsp vanilla essence
  • decorations!

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 190c
  2. Beat the butter and sugar together until smooth
  3. Add in the vanilla essence
  4. stir in the flour until combined
  5. turn onto a clean work surface and knead the dough to bring the mixture into a smooth ball
  6. with a rolling pin, roll out the dough until it is 1cm thick
  7. Using your bone cutter, cut out shaps and place them onto the baking tray
  8. Bake for 15 mins
  9. Once cool, decorate!
