A recipe to make delicious vanilla-cupcakes

Ingredients

  • 8oz margarine
  • 8oz caster sugar
  • 8oz self raising flour
  • 4 eggs

Method

  1. preheat the oven to 180c
  2. Beat the sugar and margarine together in a bowl
  3. beat the eggs in a cup
  4. add the eggs a bit at a time, beating into the margarine and sugar mix
  5. fold in the flour
  6. spoon the mixture into cupcake cases until the are 3/4 full
  7. bake for 20 minutes
